# M-004 Core Vision

**Release:** v0.1 Foundation

**Status:** ✅ Complete

---

# Objective

Define the long-term mission and engineering philosophy behind Cougar OS.

---

# Vision

Cougar OS is designed to become an AI Operating System capable of powering intelligent organizations through modular AI workforces, enterprise memory, secure governance, and autonomous business operations.

---

# Engineering Philosophy

The platform should:

- Scale through architecture.
- Preserve organizational knowledge.
- Prioritize security.
- Encourage modular development.
- Remain understandable as complexity grows.

---

# Long-Term Goals

Future releases will build toward:

- Enterprise Memory
- AI Workforce
- Private Lane
- Organizational Intelligence
- Trading Intelligence
- Autonomous Enterprises

---

# Outcome

Every engineering decision made after this milestone should support the long-term vision established here.
