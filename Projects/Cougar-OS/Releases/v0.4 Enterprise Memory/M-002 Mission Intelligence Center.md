---

# Engineering Decision

Mission Intelligence should remain separate from Workforce Management.

The workforce executes missions.

Mission Intelligence analyzes and presents them.

This separation allows both systems to evolve independently.

---

# Outcome

Leadership can now understand organizational progress from a centralized intelligence interface.
