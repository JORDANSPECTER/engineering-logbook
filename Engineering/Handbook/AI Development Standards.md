# AI Development Standards

---

# Purpose

Artificial Intelligence is the core capability of Cougar OS.

These standards define how AI systems should be designed and implemented throughout the platform.

---

# AI Principles

## AI Should Have Purpose

Every AI capability must have a clearly defined responsibility.

Avoid creating AI simply because it is technically possible.

---

## Organizational AI

AI should function as part of an intelligent organization rather than isolated assistants.

Examples include:

- Executive Lanes
- AI Workforce
- Private Lane
- Mission Nodes

---

## Explainability

AI decisions should be understandable whenever practical.

Users should understand why recommendations are made.

---

## Human Oversight

AI supports decision-making.

Humans retain final authority over significant organizational actions.

---

## Security First

AI systems must respect:

- Identity
- Permissions
- Organizational boundaries
- Privacy
- Memory governance

---

## Memory

AI should learn from approved organizational knowledge.

Memory must remain governed, traceable, and explainable.

---

## Reusability

Shared AI capabilities should become platform services rather than duplicated implementations.

---

# AI Philosophy

Cougar does not build AI to replace people.

Cougar builds AI to strengthen organizations.
