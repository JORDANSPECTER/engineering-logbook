# Portfolio Summary

Project Cougar is an enterprise AI platform designed around the concept of organizational intelligence.

Unlike traditional software applications, Cougar focuses on:

- AI Workforce orchestration
- Organizational memory
- Executive decision support
- Knowledge graphs
- Enterprise runtime services
- AI governance
- Intelligent organizational workflows

The platform is being developed using production engineering practices, comprehensive documentation, and architectural governance.
